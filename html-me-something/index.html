European Soccer Pilgrimage of 2019

In March of 2019, my brother, nephew, and I embarked in a 10-day journey through Europe to attend soccer games.

Chelsea vs Wolverhampton (Stamford Bridge)

Our first stop was in London, England, were we attended Stamford Bridge, commonly referred to as the Bridge. It is the home of Premier League club Chelsea.

The match ended with a 1-1 Draw.

Juventus vs Atletico Madrid (Allianz Stadium)

Next stop, Turin, Italy. Little did we know that the next game we attended, would go down as one of the best comebacks in UEFA Champions League history.

Cristiano Ronaldo, considered one of the best soccer players in the world, scored a hat-trick and helped Juventus turn a 0-2 into a 3-2 victory over Atletico Madrid.

Milan vs Inter Milan (San Siro Stadium)

Last stop, Milan, Italy. Commonly known as The Derby della Madonnina, this soccer match featured two world re-known Italian soccer clubs that share an intense rivalry.

The match ended with a 3-2 Inter Milan win over Milan.
